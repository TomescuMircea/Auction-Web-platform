Auctioning objects from around the house to get some extra money.

This section provides information on how to effectively auction an object that you no longer want to have.

What to auction

  • Valuable items that you want to obtain money on.
  • Objects that you no longer want to have in your house.
  • Objects bought that you don't like.
  • Any other type of object that you want to sell.

What Information to Include

  • The title of the auction.
  • The price you want to obtain for the object (minimum price).
  • A large description for your auction.
  • The day when the auction will end (the auction will be active until that day).
  • The time when the auction will end (the auction will be active until that time).

How to auction

  • Visit our auction section and fill out the online form.
  • You can also call us directly at [phone number] (during business hours).
